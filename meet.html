Donald Miller

Donald might look young, but he has more than 15 years of coding experience under his belt. He built his own surfing robot when he was only twelve years old, and even got a grant from Boston Dynamics to do further research and development on surfing robotics. A code instructor and surf instructor combined.

Gandalf Gallagher

Gandalf is something of a legend in the surfing community, as he goes way back to the early days of surfing. He actually taught the Beach Boys how to surf, and he was humming a little tune to himself during his lessons with them. That little tune went on to be the inspiration for "Surfing the USA".

David Mulberry

David is a camp co-ordinator who oversees all of the behind the scenes tasks, the timetables and the charts, the liasoning with parents and children to ensure that everything is going smoothly, and provides vital support to surf and code instructors.

Sally "Shredder" Simpson

Sally is one of the finest surf instructors that the world has ever seen. She has won the world surfing championships twice now, but has since decided that she wants to focus on teaching future generations of little shredders.
